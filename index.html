Odin Recipes

Introduction

Odin, King of Asgard was a connoisseur of spices. He discovered this when he was exploring Midgard. Although he set up a humble abode in Scandinavia, he enjoyed the rich coriander, cumin, caraway, cardamom, chilis, cinnamon, and capsicum. Traveling in the various areas of the British Raj in the New Imperialist era, he compiled this list of recipes. The first recipe he compiled was one for chana dal.
